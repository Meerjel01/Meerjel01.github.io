The Converted Human Race



These once-human creatures where turned into monsters by an evil force from outside of the Earth.

They possess the abilities of the human race and are also even more intelligent and ferocious. For an exception of before, they follow natural laws more than civil ones. They're dedicated to their dominance and want to grow a large imperium by wiping out one civilization after the other.

A Female and a Male


In the age where Humanity where near star traveling, the Converted had the technology to fly through space and wage war on other advanced species. With their added knowledge they invented better weapons and gear for warfare and growth. The force that changed them from the start was a single creature known as the Wrymme'Geftr, a piece of meat with immense powers.
